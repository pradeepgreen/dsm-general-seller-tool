DSM Will be calculated for ROR Hydro Seller as per following norms

NOT LINKED TO FREQUENCY - Charges based on volume limits only
Volume Limits: VL1 = 10% DGS or 100 MW | VL2 = 15% DGS or 150 MW (whichever is less)
 
OVER-INJECTION CHARGES (Receivable by Seller)
Deviation Level	Rate	Example (RR=₹2.80/kWh)	Remarks
Within VL1	@ RR	₹2.80/kWh	Full payment within limit
Beyond VL1	@ Zero	₹0.00/kWh	No payment beyond VL1
 
UNDER-INJECTION CHARGES (Payable by Seller)
Deviation Level	Rate	Example (RR=₹2.80/kWh)	Remarks
Within VL1	@ RR	₹2.80/kWh	Standard rate within limit
Beyond VL1 up to VL2	@ 105% RR	₹2.94/kWh	Moderate penalty
Beyond VL2	@ 110% RR	₹3.08/kWh	Higher penalty beyond VL2
 
KEY FEATURES
Feature	Description
Frequency Link	NO - Charges NOT linked to grid frequency
Rationale	Limited controllability due to water flow dependency
Volume Limits	Two-tier: VL1 (10%/100MW) and VL2 (15%/150MW)
Penalty Level	Moderate (105-110% RR) recognizing operational constraints
Change from 2022	Maintained RR linkage only (removed NR linkage)

DSM Will be calculated for WIND-SOLAR (WS) SELLERS - DSM CHARGES (CERC Regulation 2024)
 
VOLUME LIMITS BY TECHNOLOGY
Generator Type	VL1	VL2	VL3	Beyond VL3
Solar / Hybrid / Aggregated	≤ 5% DWS	5-10% DWS	10-20% DWS	> 20% DWS
Wind	≤ 10% DWS	10-15% DWS	15-25% DWS	> 25% DWS
 
OVER-INJECTION CHARGES (Receivable by Seller)
Volume Limit	Rate	Solar Example (₹3.50/kWh)	Wind Example (₹4.20/kWh)	Remarks
VL1	@ 100% Contract Rate	₹3.50/kWh	₹4.20/kWh	Full contract rate
VL2	@ 90% Contract Rate	₹3.15/kWh	₹3.78/kWh	10% reduction
VL3	@ 50% Contract Rate	₹1.75/kWh	₹2.10/kWh	50% reduction
Beyond VL3	@ Zero	₹0.00/kWh	₹0.00/kWh	No payment
 
UNDER-INJECTION CHARGES (Payable by Seller)
Volume Limit	Rate	Solar Example (₹3.50/kWh)	Wind Example (₹4.20/kWh)	Remarks
VL1	@ 100% Contract Rate	₹3.50/kWh	₹4.20/kWh	Standard rate
VL2	@ 110% Contract Rate	₹3.85/kWh	₹4.62/kWh	10% penalty
VL3	@ 150% Contract Rate	₹5.25/kWh	₹6.30/kWh	50% penalty
Beyond VL3	@ 200% Contract Rate	₹7.00/kWh	₹8.40/kWh	Maximum penalty
 
DEVIATION CALCULATION FORMULA
Period	Formula
Aug 2024 - Mar 2026	DWS (%) = 100 × [(Actual - Scheduled) / Available Capacity]
From Apr 2026	DWS (%) = 100 × [(Actual - Scheduled) / (X% × Available + (100-X)% × Scheduled)]
 
KEY FEATURES
Feature	Description
Frequency Link	NO - Charges NOT linked to grid frequency
Pricing Structure	SYMMETRICAL - Prevents gaming behavior (over-scheduling)
Aggregation	Through Qualified Coordinating Agency (QCA) at pooling stations
Tolerance Reduction	Solar: ±10% to ±5% | Wind: ±15% to ±10% (due to aggregation benefits)
Change from 2022	Shifted from asymmetric to symmetric pricing
Available Capacity	Capacity capable of generating at that time block (not installed capacity)
