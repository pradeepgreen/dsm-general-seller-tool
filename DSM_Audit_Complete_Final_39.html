⚡ Frequency Benefit Analysis — Over Injection during Low Frequency

Slots where frequency was between 49.90 Hz and 49.97 Hz (low frequency band)

How CERC DSM rewards Over Injection during Low Frequency:
When grid frequency falls between 49.90 Hz and 49.97 Hz, a generator that over-injects is helping stabilise the grid. CERC DSM rewards this by applying a multiplier > 1.0 on Slab 1 receivable (interpolated between 1.0 at 49.97 Hz and 1.15 at 49.90 Hz).
Bonus = Actual Receivable − Reference Receivable (i.e., the extra amount earned above the normal rate).
Slab 2 receivable remains zero (multiplier = 0) until frequency drops below 49.90 Hz where multiplier becomes −0.1 (payable).

Manual Entry — Step-by-Step
1
Company Name & Week — Appears as title in Executive Summary and all exports.
2
Date & Time Slot — Date picker + dropdown of all 96 slots (00:00–23:45).
3
Scheduled MWh & Actual MWh — Actual < Scheduled = UI. Actual > Scheduled = OI.
4

5
Click ⚙ Process Entry — Auto-switches to Executive Summary.

5
Click ⚙ Generate Audit Report — All tabs populated automatically.

— Interpretation Guide —

DSM Basics
Deviation = Actual − Scheduled  |  Negative=UI (charge)  |  Positive=OI (recv or charge)
Penalty/Saving = Actual DSM − Value@Ref  (Positive=penalty, Negative=saving)
ScenarioFrequencyImpact
UIAnyDSM Charge payable
OI≥50.05 HzDSM Charge payable
OI49.90–50.04Benefit receivable
OI<49.90 HzHigh benefit — premium multiplier

KPI Cards — All amounts in Lakhs (₹X.XX L)
CardInterpretation
UI DSM PayableTotal DSM charge for all UI blocks.
OI DSM ReceivableTotal DSM benefit for OI blocks.
Total PenaltyDSM cost exceeding PPA reference rate.
Revenue LossOI benefit foregone at high frequency.
Net DSM PayableUI Payable − OI Receivable. ↳ sub-lines show both.
Deviation SlotsUI+OI count (number, not ₹).
🔴
Under Injection

Q1=within 10% deviation. Q2=beyond 10%. Penalty/Saving=Actual DSM − Value@Ref. Red=Penalty (low freq, multiplier>1). Green=Saving (high freq, multiplier<1). Tables show Indian comma amounts.

🟢
Over Injection

OI <50.05 Hz = benefit receivable. OI ≥50.05 Hz = charge payable. Zero Pay Loss = revenue foregone at high-frequency surplus-grid slots.

⚠
Material Deviations

Blocks where Penalty/Loss > threshold (default ₹5,000). Type Audit Remarks in last column — included in all exports. Each block = CERC Reg. 6A compliance event.

📋
Full Annexure

Complete slot-level ledger. Positive=OI receivable. Negative=UI payable. Cross-verify: sum of Penalty/Loss = Total Penalty + Revenue Loss KPI. Export Excel for SLDC/RLDC.
